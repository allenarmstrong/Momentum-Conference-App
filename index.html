Registration/Parking/Amenities

Free Parking

A parking pass will be provided prior to the conference and parking is free if displayed on the car dash. Valet parking is not reimbursable.

Rooms and Amenities

The Hilton features 1,400 guest rooms and more than 175,000 square feet of meeting space. It’s located at the crossroads of I-4, the Beachline Expressway (528) and International Drive. The guest rooms include two queen size beds, an in-room refrigerator, desk areas with nesting tables that offer two people comfortable work space, ergonomic desk chairs, programmable safes and large LCD TVs. Internet service will be included. The hotel has a health club and is providing complimentary access for FLVS employees during our stay.

Internet

You will have free wireless access in your rooms as long as you have your promotional code which will be provided to you UPON check in. Per the hotel, a promotional code will be provided with username and password for the entire group. You will select NEW, enter the promotional code and then proceed to enter your last name and room number. This will work throughout the hotel and meeting rooms.

Emergencies

The Hilton Orlando is fully prepared to handle different types of situations to assist our guests. The following is information on our emergency procedures:

  • The hotel internal emergency number is 66.
  • The hotel has an emergency response team 24 hours a day. In the event of an emergency, calling the emergency number 66 will initiate the appropriate response.
  • Our Security Department, as well as a small number of other employees, is trained in CPR and First Aid.
  • Emergency evacuation routes and procedures are located on the inside of all guest room doors.

IT Hub

The Client Services department will be hosting the IT Hub which will be the center of all support issues for your systems. The entire Client Services Team will be there to meet you and assist in your IT journey. The IT Hub will be hosting three different areas: Systems, Solutions and Experiences.

  • Systems will be the location to go if you have any issues with your laptops. Replacing, cleaning, and how to use your system will be the services provided.
  • Solutions will be provided by our Applications Services team to address VSA, Educator and other software products. Providing solutions to your workflow and resolving issues will be our focus!
  • Experiences will introduce new tools to make your workflow easier. From password management, file storage and instant messaging, Experiences will allow you to have a more productive day!

IT Hub Session times and locations are located in the agenda.

Travel

As you know, you have been given the option of having a room at the Hilton during the conference. The Hilton will be considered our work location for the dates of the conference. Therefore, if you live within a 50 mile radius of the Hilton, mileage will not be reimbursed. Even if you opt not to stay at the hotel, we will not reimburse for travel to and from the hotel (this is no different than if you were to drive to the VLC on those dates).

Parking is provided at no cost at the hotel. Most of your meals are also provided. For those meals not provided, you may claim per diem if you are staying overnight. If you choose to commute, that is your option. However, we will not reimburse for the daily mileage.

As launched in late August, the Blueprint to Common Core helps all staff at FLVS connect the lines between the various moving parts of our Common Core implementation.
